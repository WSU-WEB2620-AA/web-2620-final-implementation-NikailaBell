ZOOTOPIA 2

"It's a whole new jungle out there."

Brave rabbit cop Judy Hopps and her slick fox partner Nick Wilde return for a brand new case that threatens to tear the city of Zootopia apart.

When a mysterious reptile syndicate begins operating in the shadows of the Marshlands District, Judy and Nick must go undercover to solve the crime. Their investigation will test their friendship and reveal secrets about the city's founding that change everything they thought they knew about predator and prey.
