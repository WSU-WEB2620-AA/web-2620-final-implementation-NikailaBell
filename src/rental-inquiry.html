Rental Terms & Conditions
The Basics
  • We have 3 total auditoriums available for rent (132 seats or 274 seats).
  • Rental Duration: 2 ½ hours total (30 minutes to gather/concessions, 2 hours for the movie).
  • Note: If your movie is longer than 2 hrs we will need to bump your start time into the 30 min gathering time! Please be aware of your rental time as we have other movies scheduled after you.
Available Times
  • Monday–Thursday evenings: 5:00pm or 7:30pm
  • Saturdays: 12:00pm and 2:30pm
  • No cancellations.
Movie Selection

You may choose any movie we are currently playing (posted the Tuesday before your rental) OR bring your own DVD/Blu-ray (No 4K).

Running Times: Generally movies are 120 min or less. If your movie is 125–170 min, we start early during gathering time. If longer than 2 ½ hours, you must book two back-to-back slots.

Please be on time, we will not start your movie late.

Rules & Regulations
  • NO OUTSIDE FOOD OR DRINK. This includes water bottles, cupcakes, treat bags, etc.
  • NO DECORATIONS inside the auditorium. Balloons are allowed in the lobby only.
  • NO PARTY ROOM. Your party is the movie!
Concessions Options

If purchasing for your group, please inform the manager of your preference:

  1. Ticket: You distribute tickets with a set dollar amount. Guests pay with tickets; you pay the total at the end.
  2. Open Tab: Guests order at a designated counter; you pay the running tab at the end.
  3. Individual: Each guest purchases their own.
Final Agreement

By booking, you agree to the following:

  • $150.00 FINE for any member of your party who climbs, dances, or places anything (including popcorn bags) on the stage.
  • Noise levels must be kept to a minimum as other movies are playing.
  • Be kind and courteous to others.
  • No Cancellations or Refunds.
