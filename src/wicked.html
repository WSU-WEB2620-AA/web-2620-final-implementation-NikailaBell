WICKED

"Everyone deserves the chance to fly."

Based on the acclaimed Broadway musical, Wicked tells the untold story of the witches of Oz. Elphaba, a young woman misunderstood because of her unusual green skin, has yet to discover her true power.

She meets Glinda, a popular young woman gilded by privilege and ambition. Their initial rivalry turns into an unlikely friendship, but the world decides to call one "good" and the other one "wicked."

Starring

Cynthia Erivo Elphaba
Ariana Grande Glinda
